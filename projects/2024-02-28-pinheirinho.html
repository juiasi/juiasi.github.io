A Lenda do Pinheirinho

“A Lenda do Pinheirinho”, which in portuguese means “The legend of the little pine tree”, is a children's book of poetry, written by Lorena Miranda Cutlak and published by João e Maria, inspired by a traditional portuguese christian tale. I illustrated this book using oil pastels and colored pencils, and the cover title was drawn in Procreate.
